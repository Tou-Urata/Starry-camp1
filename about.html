Our Stargazing Story

Reconnecting humanity with the cosmic wonder above us

Why We Look Up

At Starry Night Camping, we believe the night sky is humanity's most precious shared inheritance.

Founded in 2015 by a group of astronomy enthusiasts, we began with a simple observation: in our modern world, we've lost touch with the celestial wonders that guided our ancestors for millennia. Light pollution now obscures the Milky Way from nearly 80% of North Americans.

We created Starry Night Camping to restore that connection - to give people of all backgrounds the opportunity to experience the awe our ancestors felt when they looked up at an unpolluted night sky.

This is more than camping. It's remembering our place in the cosmos.

Our Guiding Light

"We exist to remind humanity that beneath the same stars, we are one family on a small, fragile planet. By bringing people together under the celestial dome, we rekindle wonder, foster connection, and inspire protection of our shared night sky heritage."

The Starry Night Team

What We Stand For

Celestial Access

We preserve and provide access to dark sky locations where the universe remains visible in all its glory. Our campsites are carefully selected for optimal stargazing conditions.

Human Connection

Under the stars, social barriers dissolve. We create spaces where people from all walks of life can share the universal experience of cosmic wonder.

Earth Stewardship

By reconnecting people with nature's night rhythms, we inspire more sustainable living and protection of our planet's delicate ecosystems.

"The nitrogen in our DNA, the calcium in our teeth, the iron in our blood, the carbon in our apple pies were made in the interiors of collapsing stars. We are made of starstuff."

— Carl Sagan
